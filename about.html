About Us

Welcome to Bunting, the ultimate destination for high-quality yet affordable artist paints and supplies! Founded with a passion for nurturing creativity and empowering artists of all levels, Bunting prides itself on providing top-notch products that inspire masterpieces. At the heart of our company is our visionary CEO, Amanda Brandt, whose unwavering dedication to quality and innovation drives everything we do. With years of experience in the art industry and a deep understanding of artists' needs, Amanda leads our team with boundless energy and a commitment to excellence. Under her guidance, Bunting continues to push boundaries, ensuring that every artist can access the tools they need to bring their visions to life. Join us on our journey to unleash creativity and transform the world, one brushstroke at a time.
